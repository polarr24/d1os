# Hudl upload day (items 14 + 5) — the reel stays alive

Recruiters bounce off stale film. The app now shows reel freshness (PATH → Recruiting Ops)
and turns red past 30 days in-season. This is the ritual that keeps it green:

1. **File the film** — run `FILM-IMPORT.ps1` (names it `YYYY-MM-DD-opponent`).
2. **Tag while fresh** — PUSH → Film Room: tag your best 3 and worst 2 plays from the game.
   The 24-hour rule from THE VAULT applies to bad film.
3. **Cut + upload** — top 3 plays to the front of the Hudl reel. Best play FIRST —
   coaches decide in the opening 10 seconds.
4. **Tell the app** — PATH → HUDL UPDATED ✓. Freshness chip resets, dossier updates.
5. **Log the game line** — PATH → Season Stat Line: carries, yards, TD, tackles.

Total: ~25 minutes, same night as film release. In-season this happens weekly —
the freshness chip is watching either way.
